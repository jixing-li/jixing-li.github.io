Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

I grew up in Chengdu, China and got my B.A. in English from Beijing Normal University , after which I went to University College London for my M.A. in Linguistics, and University of Oxford for my M.Sc. in Experimental Psychology. I started as a PhD student in Linguisitcs at Cornell in 2013. Highlights of my awards and activities are below.
